The goal of the Lost Rover Game is to fix the rovers three parts: the engine, the cabin, and the exhaust.
Simply hover over a broken part in order to find the to repair it.
Pick up items by hovering over them and clicking the "Pick up" button.
If the item is not on the screen, the player may use the portals to explore for parts.
The "Show the way back" button highlights the portal the rove came through and leads back to the ship.
Once all items in a task have been collected, hover over the desired part and click "Perform task" to fix it.
Fix all three parts and you win!
